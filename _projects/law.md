description: This project analyzes how the GDPR framework applies to Italian associations, focusing on the challenges they face in managing personal data and drafting privacy policies. The study reviews the evolution of European data protection legislation and examines key GDPR principles, roles, and data subject rights. A case study of a cultural association organizing the Poplar Festival illustrates how privacy policies are implemented in practice when collecting volunteer data. The analysis highlights the difficulties small associations encounter in complying with complex data protection regulations.
